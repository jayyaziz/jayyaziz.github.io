Special Menu

Nando's Menu

On the menu at Nando's is chicken, chicken, and more chicken! A quarter, half or whole chicken, wings or chicken tenders - the choice is yours. There is a selection of burgers and wraps, including some vegetarian options. For starters, there are various options including chicken skewers, chicken livers with Portuguese roll or sweet potato chips. Some of the most popular main dishes include:

  • Cataplana Originale - PERi-PERi chicken with Mediterranean rice and cataplana sauce
  • Chicken hot pot - tender pulled chicken with spicy rice and tomato relish
  • Sharing platters - a whole chicken and sides for up to six people
  • Chicken breast burger - on a Portuguese roll with PERinaise, the spicy mayonnaise
  • A range of sides complements the chicken dishes perfectly, including: PERi-PERi Chips, Spicy Rice, Chargrilled Vegetables, Side Salad

Finish off your meal with a cold drink and some extra PERi-PERi sauces if you like. Nando's also offers delicious desserts, such as PERi-PERi Brownies, infused with the famous sauce and topped with chilli flakes or creamy Caramel Cheesecake with muscovado sugar. Order chicken for delivery from foodpanda and you will soon be enjoying Nando's in the comfort of your own home, it's ideal for a cosy night in or a party with friends.
